The History of Falafel

Falafel's origins are debated, but it is widely believed to have originated in Egypt as "ta'ameya," a bean-based dish. Over time, falafel spread across the Middle East, and the chickpea version became the most popular. Today, falafel is loved worldwide, served as a street food snack or a main dish in restaurants.
